Frequently Asked Questions

  • What does the pass include ?

    Your ticket unlocks a treasure trove of excitement and thrills, literally. Here are the list of things that are included in your ticket: Contact Improvisation Masterclass by Manas Yellapantula Q/A session by a Mental Health Practitioner on Body Imaging Showcase by Artists all across the city Sundowner at 'TBD'

  • What is PINK'D?

    It is a series of inclusive events that celebrate art through art and bring communities together. PINK'D offers two dynamic masterclasses that not only provide a unique learning experience but also support a meaningful cause. The event includes a showcase of talented performers followed by a sundowner. We're bringing World Dance Day to India; Come, get Pink'd!

  • Will I be able to book my ticket on the venue?

    You can book your ticket on the venue, however the price will vary

  • How many people can enter with one ticket and are there any age restrictions for the event ?

    One ticket is only valid for one person. There will be a separate ticket for people below the age of 18, which will include all events, except the sundowner. There are no age restrictions for the masterclasses or the showcase.
